## 🔐 Personnel Security Controls

---

### 📘 Concept

Personnel security ensures individuals are **trusted, properly vetted, and restricted to defined access boundaries**.

The goal is to **minimize insider threat and prevent abuse of privilege** through controlled access and accountability.

---

### 🧩 Core Principles

**Separation of Duties (SoD)**  
Divides **critical tasks among multiple individuals** to prevent fraud, abuse, or single points of failure.  
No single individual should control an entire sensitive process.

**Least Privilege**  
Grants users **only the minimum level of access required** to perform their job.  
Access is limited in **scope, duration, and capability**.

**Need to Know**  
Restricts access based on **necessity for specific information**, not just role or clearance level.  
Even authorized users only access **what is required for their task**.

---

### ⚠️ Critical Distinction

Each control addresses a different risk:

<p align="center"><strong>Separation of Duties = Split Responsibility  
Least Privilege = Limit Access  
Need to Know = Limit Information Exposure</strong></p>

All three must work **together** to effectively reduce risk.

---

### 🎯 Why This Matters (CISSP Context)

Spans **Security and Risk Management (Domain 1)** and **Identity and Access Management (Domain 5)**.

These controls directly mitigate:

- Insider threats  
- Privilege abuse  
- Fraud and unauthorized actions  
- Accidental misuse of access  

Without these controls:

- Users gain excessive control  
- Sensitive data is overexposed  
- Critical processes lack accountability  

CISSP questions will often require you to **reduce access scope or divide responsibilities** rather than add new controls.

---

### 🧠 CISSP Decision Lens

When evaluating a scenario:

1. Is one person performing **too many critical functions**? → Apply Separation of Duties  
2. Does a user have **more access than required**? → Apply Least Privilege  
3. Does a user have access to **unnecessary data**? → Apply Need to Know  

Default mindset:

**Reduce access and distribute control**

---

### 🚨 Exam Trap

Granting **broad or excessive access** for convenience, efficiency, or speed of operations.

---

### ✅ Exam Takeaway

**Limit access and divide responsibilities to reduce risk.**
