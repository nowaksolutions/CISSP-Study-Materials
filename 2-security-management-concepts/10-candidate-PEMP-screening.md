## 🔐 Candidate Screening and Pre-Employment Controls

---

### 📘 Concept

Pre-employment controls ensure candidates are **properly vetted and legally bound** before being granted access to systems or data.

The objective is to **verify trustworthiness before trust is granted**, reducing risk **before onboarding begins**.

---

### 🧩 Screening Process (Pre-Employment Due Diligence)

**Define Job Requirements**  
Establish required **knowledge, skills, and attributes (KSAs)** aligned to the role.

**Preliminary Screening**  
Use initial questions to **filter unqualified or high-risk candidates**.

**Background Review**  
Evaluate **resumes, CVs, references, and employment history** for accuracy and consistency.

**Background Checks and Interviews**  
Conduct **formal verification and assessment** to validate identity, qualifications, and suitability.

---

### ⚖️ Legal and Policy Controls

These establish **expectations, accountability, and enforceability**:

- **Employment Agreements** – Define roles, responsibilities, and obligations  
- **Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs)** – Protect sensitive information  
- **Non-Compete Clauses** – Restrict competitive conflicts (where legally enforceable)  
- **Acceptable Use Policies (AUPs)** – Define proper system usage  
- **Conflict of Interest Policies** – Prevent biased or risky behavior  
- **Gift Policies** – Reduce bribery and undue influence risk  

---

### ⚠️ Critical Principle

**Trust must be verified before access is granted.**

Access should **never be provisioned** until screening and legal controls are complete.

<p align="center"><strong>Verify → Approve → Grant Access</strong></p>

---

### 🎯 Why This Matters (CISSP Context)

Falls under **Security and Risk Management (Domain 1)** and addresses **pre-employment insider threat risk**.

Failure in screening leads to:

- Unauthorized or malicious insiders  
- Fraud, data theft, or sabotage  
- Legal and compliance exposure  

CISSP questions will test your ability to apply **due diligence before granting access or trust**.

---

### 🧠 CISSP Decision Lens

When evaluating a scenario:

1. Has the individual been **properly vetted and verified**?  
2. Are **legal agreements and policies in place**?  
3. Has **access been withheld until validation is complete**?  

Default mindset:

**No validation → No access**

---

### 🚨 Exam Trap

Skipping or weakening **screening and verification** due to urgency, staffing shortages, or business pressure.

---

### ✅ Exam Takeaway

**Strong hiring controls reduce insider threats before they start.**
